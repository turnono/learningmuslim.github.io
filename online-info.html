Learning Muslim Online Courses

An introduction to Arabic for students of the Quraan

Build a practical foundation in Grammar And Morphology to fast track your journey into the Quran via it's original language. This course is designed for anyone having the basic ability to read the Qur'aan and would like to embark on the next step of understanding what they are reading.

Course Details

This course includes 30 lessons, each comprising of 3 components; Nahw (Grammar), Sarf (Morphology) and an Arabic reader. Each component includes an audio/visual aspect followed by a graded exercise. This course is facilitated by three of our Teachers. This syllabus is designed to maximise the understanding of core concepts in the shortest time possible.
Duration: 1 month mininum but students have lifetime access, so you may complete it at your own pace.
Start Date: Today
Material: Pdf copies of our books, access to our PPT slides, access to our online homework web-app.
End Date: work at your own pace!
Platform: Google Classroom
Skill: beginners having the ability to read Quraan as well as advanced
Cost: R2500, includes course material
Language: English
Facilators: Ml Bilal, Ml Abdur rahman, Ml Abdullah
Certification: Yes

How can I join the program?
This program is specifically for people who want to understand Arabic as it relates to the Quran and the Islamic legacy.

This is a fully online course! Login and complete assignments whenever you wish! No mandatory live sessions to attend!
